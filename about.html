    • A new generation of winemakers

    Incorporated on the 8th of March 1994, Eastern Distilleries and Food Industries limited has grown over the years to become a force to reckon with in the beverage and spirit industry in Nigeria.Eastern Distilleries started off small, in the Harbour Industrial Layout of Onitsha at fringes of the river Niger as a growing manufacturing firm with just a handful of alcoholic brands in its portfolio. Today, and just after more than two decades, the company has expanded to become a foremost manufacturer of premium alcoholic and non -alcoholic beverages that will stand shoulder high among their contemporaries. This is owing to the doggedness of the management of Eastern Distilleries whose mantra has always been taking a poise towards ensuring that the company remains consistent in her promises of unbeatable quality and consistency.

    BEST QUALITY WINE

    Eastern Distilleries on the 15th of December 2015, launched the first made in Nigeria brandy named, Brandy Almondia; a wholly made in Nigerian premium brandy crafted for the upwardly mobile individuals who have a penchant for class, status and style. At the moment, the product remains a top of the mind item in the minds of brandy connoisseurs. The product received a wide acceptance among the youth and saw Emmanuel Ikubese, the former Mr. Nigeria as its first brand ambassador. After that feat, EDFIhas not rested on its oars.

    It keeps working round the clock to ensure that it keeps all promises made to its wide network of consumers, stakeholders and loyalists. Our knack for quality propelled us to wade into the Bitters market. Taking a whole decade, the company came up with an indisputable real herbal bitters drink known as BallamourBitters; a favourite alcoholic beverage among Bitters enthusiasts. We would have to resist the temptation of attributing any therapeutic effects of our products due to the presence of alcoholic components in the products.

    Nevertheless, our bitters has received innumerable commendations from its myriads of consumers as to its originality, consistency and overall product satisfaction. On the 1st of July, 2021, the company unveiled Whisky Elegamon; the latest entrant into the company’s premium brands SKU. The product joins other foremost brands in the ever- growing dominance of EDFI in the spirit industry, especially when quality and consistency are of topmost considerations.Whisky Elegamon was conceived for connoisseurs who have a penchant for something exclusive that reflects their innate desires to associate with that which represents class, status and style.

    In recognition of the quality-consistency of the company, the majority of the company’s brands have received the endorsement of the standards organization of Nigeria, National Agency for Food and Drug Administration and Control, NAFDAC among other regulatory agencies. Easter Distilleries is a wholly Nigerian company established for distilling, blending, packaging and distribution of both alcoholic and non-alcoholic beverages for Nigeria and beyond..

    In Eastern Distilleries and Food Industries Limited, we are committed to quality, which is a hallmark we inherited from our parent company. Quality is revealed in every segment of our manufacturing processes. Our products are manufactured under great hygienic environment and conditions, using modern machineries. To support this commitment to quality and value for both the stakeholders and customers, we have over the years, trained and equipped a team of researchers, who with their diverse knowledge in distilling, blending and packaging have come out with finest, top-quality beverages.

    Our Research and Development department plays a vital role on our brand’s development. Through their efforts and achievements, we are constantly developing new products, tried and tested brands, to meet changing tastes, lifestyle and expectations of our customers
